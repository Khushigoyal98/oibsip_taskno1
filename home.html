Kalam Prints

Kalam is a one stop destination for all your stationary needs. Kalam is a new generation startup idea to get all your stationary needs fulfilled where one would be be able to buy, resell and get their documents printed under one umbrella.The key feature of the Kalam would be to provide online buying and printing services where one can directly upload their documents which they intend to be printed according to their specifications like size of paper, no. of papers to be printed, colored or monochrome, etc. This exclusive 'ACROPOLIAN' store also allows students to resell their stationary items at nominal prices.
